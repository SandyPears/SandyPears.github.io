Hi, I’m Cordelia! I’m a full-stack developer with a passion for turning ideas into functional, user-friendly applications. I enjoy the process of bringing concepts to life, whether it’s building intuitive interfaces or creating the systems behind them. Problem-solving and continuous learning keep me motivated, and I’m always excited to take on new challenges.

Currently, I'm a freelance web developer, creating unique and practical websites using platforms like Squarespace and WordPress. I collaborate closely with clients to ensure each design reflects their vision, with a strong focus on user experience through intuitive navigation, mobile responsiveness, and useful features.

I recently completed a Certificate IV in IT (Programming) and a Diploma in IT (Advanced Programming) through TAFE, where I gained hands-on experience in full-stack development, team collaboration, and managing all stages of the project lifecycle.

In my free time, you’ll usually find me sketching, playing Magic: The Gathering, or volunteering with my local Scout group.
