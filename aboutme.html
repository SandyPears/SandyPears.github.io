Hi, I’m Cordelia! I’m a full-stack developer with a passion for turning ideas into functional, user-friendly applications. I enjoy the process of bringing concepts to life, whether it’s building intuitive interfaces or creating the systems behind them. Problem-solving and continuous learning keep me motivated, and I’m always excited to take on new challenges.

In 2023, while completing my Cert 4 in Screen and Media, I realised that it wasn’t the path I wanted to take. After considering my options, I decided to pursue something in IT, as it was a field that had always come somewhat naturally to me. After further consideration, programming stood out to me because of its problem-solving aspects and creativity. I made the switch and began my studies, completing my Cert 4 in IT (Programming) and Diploma in IT (Advanced Programming) at TAFE in 2024.

Outside of development, I’ve been a proud member of Scouts NSW since 2011. Currently, I’m a trainee Joey Scout Leader, where I have the opportunity to guide and inspire young children. Being involved in scouting has taught me valuable skills in leadership, teamwork, and communication, which I believe have helped shape me both personally and professionally.

In my spare time, I enjoy playing board games and trading card games (TCGs), especially Magic: The Gathering. These hobbies have introduced me to fantastic communities where strategy and creativity come together, and I love the social and competitive aspects they offer. I also have a passion for both digital and traditional art, which allows me to explore my creative side and express myself in various ways. These hobbies not only help me unwind but also contribute to my problem-solving and creative thinking skills, which I bring to my work as a developer.
